The Momos Recipe

Ingredients

For Dough:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • 1 tsp oil (optional)

For Filling:

  • 1 cup cabbage (finely chopped/grated)
  • ½ cup carrot (grated)
  • ¼ cup capsicum (finely chopped)
  • ¼ cup onion or spring onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped) – optional
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough

    In a bowl, mix flour and salt. Add water little by little and knead into a soft, smooth dough. Cover with a damp cloth and rest for 30 minutes.

  2. Make the Filling

    Heat oil in a pan. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a few seconds. Add onion, green chili, and cook until soft. Add cabbage, carrot, and capsicum. Sauté on high heat for 2–3 minutes (don’t overcook). Add soy sauce, pepper, and salt. Mix well. Let the filling cool completely.

  3. Shape the Momos

    Divide dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin circle (like a puri), about 3-inch diameter. Place 1 tbsp of filling in the center. Fold and pleat to seal the edges (half-moon or round shape).

  4. Steam the Momos

    Place momos in a steamer greased with oil or lined with cabbage leaves. Steam for 10–12 minutes on medium heat, until the outer cover looks transparent and cooked.

    Serve With:

  • Spicy red chutney (tomato + red chili-based)
  • Soy sauce or chili garlic sauce
